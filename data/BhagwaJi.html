Bhagwa And Its Importance

So firstly let's understand about the word "Bhagwa" that what does it means , Where did this Word Derived and most Important What Does it Signifies and also it's Value and Importance in Ancient India/World.
Bhagwa is a special color of a object specially piece of cloth. It is also worn by Saints/Gurus which shows their devotion towards the religion. The word Bhagwa is taken by a sanskrit word Bhagwat meaning Holy, Bhagwa is also said to be connected with The Hinduism. It is also considered as the holy color of Hinduism . Bhagwa also represents Purity of a substance.
Bhagwa is the also The Symbol of Six Things :
  • All Kinds Of Wealth
  • Dharma
  • Prosperity
  • Knowledge
  • Reputation
  • Detachment
